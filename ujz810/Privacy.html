Privacy Policy

Remote & Hybrid Jobs UK

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Remote & Hybrid Jobs UK collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our job search platform and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use our platform, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number)
  • Professional information (CV/resume, work experience, skills, qualifications)
  • Job preferences (location, salary expectations, job type preferences)
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Communication records with our platform and employers

1.2 Technical Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Website usage data and analytics
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Search queries and platform interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Job Matching: To connect you with relevant remote and hybrid job opportunities
  • Communication: To send job alerts, platform updates, and respond to inquiries
  • Profile Creation: To create and maintain your job seeker profile
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Employer Services: To facilitate connections between job seekers and employers
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 With Employers

When you apply for jobs or express interest in positions, we may share your profile information with relevant employers. This includes your CV, contact details, and job preferences.

3.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party providers who assist us with:

  • Email delivery and communication services
  • Analytics and website performance monitoring
  • Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Payment processing (if applicable)
  • Customer support services

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Active profiles: Until you delete your account or request removal
  • Communication records: Up to 7 years for legal compliance
  • Analytics data: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
  • Inactive accounts: May be deleted after 2 years of inactivity

5. Your Rights (GDPR & UK Data Protection)

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Provide personalized job recommendations
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Staff training on data protection practices
  • Incident response procedures

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the UK/EEA. When we do this, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Binding Corporate Rules
  • Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites, including employer websites and job application portals. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices on our platform

Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us:

Email: privacy@remotejobsuk.com
